<p><strong>The Ceramic Electrostatic Chucks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceramic Electrostatic Chucks market includes two primary types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks utilize a uniform electric field to hold substrates in place, offering strong clamping forces suitable for various applications. JR Type Electrostatic Chucks employ a method that generates adhesion through the interaction of charged plates and the dielectric layer on the substrate, leading to highly efficient and precise handling. Both types are critical in semiconductor manufacturing for improved processing accuracy.</p></p>
