<p><strong>The Organ Transplant Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Molecular Assay Technologies</li><li>Non-molecular Assay Technologies</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organ transplant diagnostics market is categorized into two main types: molecular assay technologies and non-molecular assay technologies. Molecular assay technologies involve techniques that analyze genetic material to assess donor-recipient compatibility and monitor transplant rejection, offering high sensitivity and specificity. Conversely, non-molecular assay technologies include traditional methods such as serological tests and histology, which evaluate immune responses and tissue compatibility. Both technologies are essential for ensuring successful organ transplants and patient outcomes through effective monitoring and diagnostics.</p></p>
