<p><p>The indoor stair lift market is primarily divided into two main types: curved and straight stair lifts. Straight stair lifts are designed for straight flight staircases, featuring simpler installation and lower costs. In contrast, curved stair lifts are custom-built to navigate staircases with bends, turns, or landings, offering greater versatility but typically at a higher price. Both types enhance mobility and accessibility, enabling individuals with limited mobility to safely and comfortably navigate different levels of a home.</p></p>
