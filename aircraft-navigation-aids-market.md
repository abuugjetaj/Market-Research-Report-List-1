<p><p>The Aircraft Navigation Aids market encompasses various technologies essential for safe aviation operations. Non-directional Beacons (NDB) provide radio signals for aircraft to determine their position. Instrument Landing Systems (ILS) assist in landing by providing precise guidance. Distance Measuring Equipment (DME) calculates the distance from a beacon. Doppler VHF Omnidirectional Range (DVOR) offers accurate azimuth information. Inertial Navigation Systems utilize onboard sensors for navigation without external references. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies and systems enhancing navigation efficiency and safety.</p></p>
