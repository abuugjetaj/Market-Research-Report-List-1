<p><p>Specialty surfactants are unique surface-active agents designed for specific applications, categorized primarily into anionic, cationic, and amphoteric types. Anionic surfactants carry a negative charge and are effective in cleansing and foaming, commonly used in detergents. Cationic surfactants, with a positive charge, excel in fabric softeners and disinfectants. Amphoteric surfactants can switch charges based on pH, offering versatility in personal care products. Additionally, bio-surfactants, derived from natural sources, provide eco-friendly options, enhancing performance while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
