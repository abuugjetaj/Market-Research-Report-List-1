<p><strong>The Chemiluminescence Immunoassay Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunofluorescence</li><li>Chemiluminescence</li><li>ELISA</li><li>Enzyme Linked Fluorescent System</li><li>Multiplexed Assay System</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemiluminescence Immunoassay Analyzer market includes several types of assays. Immunofluorescence uses fluorescent dyes to detect antibodies, while chemiluminescence relies on light emission during reactions for sensitivity. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) detects proteins via enzyme-linked antibodies. The Enzyme Linked Fluorescent System combines enzyme activity with fluorescence detection. Multiplexed Assay Systems allow simultaneous detection of multiple targets in a single sample. Lastly, Radioimmunoassay utilizes radioactive isotopes for quantifying antigens or hormones, offering high sensitivity in diagnostics.</p></p>
