<p><p>The Augmented Bone Graft market comprises three primary types: Allografts, Bone Graft Substitutes, and Cell-based Matrices. Allografts utilize donated human bone to promote healing in skeletal injuries. Bone Graft Substitutes are synthetic or natural materials designed to stimulate bone growth, often featuring osteoconductive properties. Cell-based Matrices incorporate living cells to enhance regenerative capabilities and support tissue integration, serving as scaffolds for new bone formation. Together, these types cater to diverse orthopedic and dental applications, addressing bone deficiencies effectively.</p></p>
