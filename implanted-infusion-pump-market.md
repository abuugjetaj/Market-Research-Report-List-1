<p><ul><li>Programmable</li><li>Nonprogrammable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Implanted infusion pumps are categorized into programmable and non-programmable types based on how they operate. Programmable infusion pumps allow healthcare providers to set specific medication dosages, schedules, and flow rates, offering flexibility and precise control for customized patient care. Non-programmable pumps, on the other hand, deliver a fixed amount of medication continuously, making them simpler but less adaptable. The choice between these types depends on the complexity of the patient's treatment needs and the desired level of control over medication delivery.</p></p>
