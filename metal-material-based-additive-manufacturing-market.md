<p><strong>The Metal Material Based Additive Manufacturing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal material-based additive manufacturing market comprises several key technologies. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) utilizes high-power lasers to fuse metal powders layer by layer, allowing for complex geometries and high-density parts. Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powder, providing excellent thermal control and producing dense components, often used in aerospace and medical sectors. Other technologies in this market include Binder Jetting and Directed Energy Deposition, each offering unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
