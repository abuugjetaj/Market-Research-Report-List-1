<p><p>The self-injection systems market comprises two primary types: fillable and pre-filled systems. Fillable systems allow users to load their own medication, offering flexibility in dosages and formulations. They are often designed for chronic conditions requiring regular administration. In contrast, pre-filled systems come pre-loaded with medication, providing convenience and ensuring accurate dosages. These systems are popular for their ease of use, especially in emergency situations or for patients with limited self-administration skills, enhancing adherence to treatment regimens.</p></p>
