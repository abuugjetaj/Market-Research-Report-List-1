<p><p>The fitness swim fins market features three main types: short blade swim fins, long blade swim fins, and monofins. Short blade fins are designed for increased agility and are ideal for various swim strokes, enhancing kick training. Long blade fins improve power and propulsion, making them suitable for endurance training and building leg strength. Monofins, used primarily in competitive and recreational fin swimming, allow swimmers to propel through the water with a dolphin-like movement, focusing on technique and efficiency.</p></p>
