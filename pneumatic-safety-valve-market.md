<p><ul><li>Surface Safety Valves</li><li>Subsurface Safety Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic safety valve market comprises two primary types: surface safety valves and subsurface safety valves. Surface safety valves are installed above ground and are designed to manage pressure and protect equipment from overpressure conditions in surface operations. In contrast, subsurface safety valves are located underground and serve to control flow and prevent uncontrolled releases of hydrocarbons from deep within reservoirs. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety and operational efficiency in oil and gas production environments.</p></p>
