<p><p>The paper pulp market encompasses various types, primarily characterized by their processing methods and fiber sources. Bleached hardwood kraft pulp (BHKP) is sourced from hardwood, offering high brightness and strength, ideal for high-quality paper products. Bleached softwood kraft pulp (BSKP), derived from softwood, provides long fibers for superior strength and durability, commonly used in packaging and printing papers. Bleached chemi-thermo mechanical pulp (BCTMP) combines chemical and mechanical processes, resulting in cost-effective pulp with good bulk and brightness, suitable for various paper formulations.</p></p>
