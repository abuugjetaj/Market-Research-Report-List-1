<p><p>The commercial vehicle motor oil market comprises two main types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil, derived from refining crude oil, offers cost-effectiveness and adequate performance for conventional engines. It requires more frequent changes due to quicker degradation. In contrast, synthetic oil is chemically engineered for superior performance, providing enhanced protection, longer change intervals, and better stability under extreme conditions. This makes synthetic oil particularly appealing for heavy-duty applications, where engine longevity and efficiency are critical.</p></p>
