<p><p>The Helmet Air Pump market is segmented into 2-channel and 4-channel types, each catering to different user needs. A 2-channel pump typically allows for the inflation of two air compartments within a helmet, focusing on simplicity and efficiency for basic models. In contrast, a 4-channel pump provides enhanced control and versatility, enabling individual inflation of four distinct air chambers. This allows for a customized fit and improved comfort, making 4-channel pumps preferred in high-end or specialized helmets.</p></p>
