<p><strong>The Electric Parking Brake Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Caliper Integrated EPB</li><li>Cable Puller EPB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric parking brake (EPB) market primarily features two types: caliper integrated EPB and cable puller EPB. Caliper integrated EPB combines the braking mechanism and electric actuator within the brake caliper, offering a compact solution that enhances vehicle aesthetics and reduces weight. In contrast, cable puller EPB uses an electric motor to pull a cable that engages the brake, providing a more traditional system that can be easier to retrofit in existing vehicles. Both types aim to improve convenience and safety.</p></p>
