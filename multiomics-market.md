<p><p>The multiomics market encompasses various approaches to analyze biological samples by integrating multiple types of omics data. Single-cell multiomics focuses on obtaining detailed information from individual cells, allowing for insights into cellular heterogeneity and specific cellular functions. In contrast, bulk multiomics aggregates data from a larger population of cells, providing a broader view of biological systems but potentially masking individual cell variations. Both types are crucial for advancing personalized medicine and improving our understanding of complex biological processes.</p></p>
