<p><ul><li>Medium Density Fibreboard</li><li>Hardboard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compressed fibreboard includes Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF) and Hardboard, both engineered wood products. MDF is made from wood fibers bonded with resin under heat and pressure, offering a smooth surface ideal for furniture, cabinetry, and molding. Hardboard, denser and stronger, is produced by compressing wood fibers at high pressure, often used in construction, furniture backing, and decorative panels. Both are cost-effective, versatile, and eco-friendly, utilizing wood waste. MDF is easier to shape, while Hardboard excels in durability and moisture resistance, catering to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
