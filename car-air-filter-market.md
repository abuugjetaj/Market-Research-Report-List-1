<p><p>The car air filter market is primarily categorized into two types: paper and oil bath filters. Paper filters, made from pleated cellulose or synthetic materials, are widely used for their affordability and efficiency in trapping dust and debris. They are disposable and require regular replacement. In contrast, oil bath filters utilize oil to capture contaminants, allowing for extended use and maintenance. These filters, often found in vintage or heavy-duty vehicles, provide superior filtration but require periodic cleaning and oil replenishment.</p></p>
