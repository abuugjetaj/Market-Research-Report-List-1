Superficial spreading melanoma is the most common type, typically appearing as irregularly shaped moles. Amelanotic melanoma lacks pigmentation, making it harder to detect. Other types encompass less common forms of melanoma, impacting the market dynamics for diagnostics and treatment options.</p></p>
