<p><strong>The Tablet Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Balanced Coil Systems</li><li>Ferrous-in-Foil Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tablet metal detector market includes two primary types: balanced coil systems and ferrous-in-foil systems. Balanced coil systems utilize two coils to create a magnetic field that identifies metal contaminants based on changes in inductance. These detectors are sensitive to various metals and provide high accuracy. Ferrous-in-foil systems are designed to detect ferrous contaminants specifically, employing advanced signal processing to differentiate between metals and product packaging. Both types play crucial roles in quality control within pharmaceutical and food industries.</p></p>
