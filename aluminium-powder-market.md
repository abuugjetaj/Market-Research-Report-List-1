<p><ul><li>Non-Spherical Aluminum Powder</li><li>Spherical Aluminum Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum powder market is primarily categorized into non-spherical and spherical aluminum powders. Non-spherical aluminum powder features irregular shapes, which enhance surface area and reactivity, making it ideal for applications in pyrotechnics, paints, and coatings. In contrast, spherical aluminum powder, known for its uniform shape, offers improved flow properties and is widely used in aerospace, automotive industries, and 3D printing. Each type serves distinct applications, benefiting from its unique physical characteristics.</p></p>
