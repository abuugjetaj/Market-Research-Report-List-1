<p><p>The aerobridge market consists of various types tailored to different airport needs. Apron Drive Aerobridges are typically used at smaller airports, utilizing a drive system on the apron for movement. Commuter Aerobridges cater to regional jets with a compact design. Dual Aerobridges support simultaneous boarding and deplaning, enhancing efficiency for larger aircraft. Nose-Loader Aerobridges connect directly to the aircraft's nose, ideal for specialized operations. Each type addresses specific operational requirements, improving passenger flow and airport functionality.</p></p>
