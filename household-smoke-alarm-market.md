<p><p>The household smoke alarm market includes various types, primarily photoelectric smoke alarms and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms use a light sensor to detect smoke particles, providing quick response to slow-burning fires. Combination smoke alarms integrate both photoelectric and ionization technologies, allowing for detection of both smoldering and flaming fires. This dual functionality enhances safety and versatility, making combination alarms popular among consumers seeking comprehensive protection for their homes. Each type serves to improve fire safety and reduce risks effectively.</p></p>
