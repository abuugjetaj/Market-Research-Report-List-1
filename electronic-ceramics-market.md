<p><ul><li>Ferroelectric</li><li>Piezoelectric</li><li>Pyroelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic ceramics market encompasses various types, including ferroelectric, piezoelectric, and pyroelectric ceramics. Ferroelectric ceramics exhibit spontaneous electrical polarization, making them useful in memory devices and capacitors. Piezoelectric ceramics generate an electric charge in response to mechanical stress, commonly used in sensors and actuators. Pyroelectric ceramics produce electric charges in response to temperature changes, finding applications in infrared sensors and energy harvesting. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing electronic components and applications across various industries.</p></p>
