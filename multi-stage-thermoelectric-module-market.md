<p><p>The multi-stage thermoelectric module market encompasses three key types: bulk thermoelectric, micro thermoelectric, and thin film thermoelectric technologies. Bulk thermoelectric modules are designed for high-power applications, utilizing larger materials for efficient heat conversion. Micro thermoelectric modules offer miniaturized solutions, ideal for compact devices, providing localized cooling or heating. Thin film thermoelectric modules feature reduced dimensions and enhanced flexibility, suited for innovative applications like wearable technology and flexible electronics, optimizing performance in constrained spaces while maintaining efficiency.</p></p>
