<p><p>The warehouse racking system market comprises various types designed for efficient storage. Drive-in racking allows high-density storage by enabling forklifts to drive directly into the racks. Selective racking offers easy access to pallets, maximizing space and flexibility. Cantilever racking is ideal for storing long or irregular items, providing adjustable shelving. Pallet flow racking utilizes gravity to move pallets through a first-in, first-out system. Additional racking systems include mobile, multi-tier, and automated solutions, catering to diverse storage needs.</p></p>
