<p><p>Paddy husker markets can be categorized into semi-pneumatic and pneumatic systems, each designed for efficient rice husking. Semi-pneumatic huskers utilize a combination of manual and mechanical elements, providing moderate automation, ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, pneumatic huskers rely on air pressure to facilitate husking, offering a more automated and high-capacity solution for larger production environments. Both systems aim to enhance husking efficiency while minimizing damage to the rice, catering to different scales of rice processing needs.</p></p>
