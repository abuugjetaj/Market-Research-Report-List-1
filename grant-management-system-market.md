<p><p>Grant Management Systems (GMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based GMS offers flexibility, scalability, and remote access, allowing organizations to manage grants efficiently without the need for extensive IT infrastructure. This model often enables automatic updates and collaborative features. However, it typically involves higher upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities.</p></p>
