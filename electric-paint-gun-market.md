<p><strong>The Electric Paint Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HVLP</li><li>LVLP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric paint gun market primarily features two types: HVLP (High Volume Low Pressure) and LVLP (Low Volume Low Pressure). HVLP guns are designed to deliver a high volume of paint at low pressure, minimizing overspray and waste, making them ideal for detailed work and efficient for large jobs. In contrast, LVLP guns use even less air, allowing for a finer spray with less overspray, making them suited for precision applications and smaller projects. Both types cater to various painting needs.</p></p>
