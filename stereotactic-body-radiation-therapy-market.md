<p><p>The Stereotactic Body Radiation Therapy (SBRT) market is primarily segmented into two types: Linear Accelerator (LINAC) machines and Proton Beam therapy. LINAC machines utilize high-energy X-rays to precisely target tumors while minimizing exposure to surrounding tissues, making them widely used due to their efficiency and versatility. In contrast, Proton Beam therapy employs charged particles for radiation delivery, offering distinct physical properties that can result in reduced damage to healthy tissue nearby. Both technologies cater to specific tumor types and patient needs.</p></p>
