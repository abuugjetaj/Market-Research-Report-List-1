<p><p>The Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) market comprises several key components: the implanted pulse generator, leads, and extensions. The implanted pulse generator is a surgically placed device that generates electrical impulses to modulate brain activity. Leads are insulated wires that transmit these impulses from the generator to specific brain regions. Extensions connect the leads to the pulse generator, often placed under the skin. Together, these components enable effective treatment of neurological disorders like Parkinson's disease and essential tremors.</p></p>
