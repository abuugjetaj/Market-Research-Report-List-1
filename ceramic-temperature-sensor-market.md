<p><p>The ceramic temperature sensor market comprises two primary types: thermistor and thermocouple temperature sensors. Thermistors are resistive devices that offer high sensitivity and accuracy at lower temperature ranges, making them ideal for applications like HVAC and medical devices. On the other hand, thermocouples are versatile sensors that can measure a broader temperature range and are widely used in industrial applications due to their robustness and quick response time. Both types cater to diverse markets, including automotive, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
