<p><p>The lymphedema diagnostics market offers various imaging techniques to assess lymphatic function. Lymphoscintigraphy uses radioactive tracers to visualize lymph flow. Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) provides detailed soft tissue images, while ultrasound imaging uses sound waves to evaluate lymphatic structures. Computed tomography (CT) delivers cross-sectional images for comprehensive assessment. X-ray lymphography visualizes lymphatic vessels using contrast agents. Bioimpedance analysis measures fluid levels in tissues, and near-infrared fluorescence imaging allows for real-time visualization of lymphatic pathways, enhancing diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
