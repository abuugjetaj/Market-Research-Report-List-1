<p><p>The coil steel market is broadly divided into hot coil steel and cold coil steel. Hot coil steel is produced by rolling steel at high temperatures, resulting in a thicker, more malleable product typically used in construction and manufacturing. In contrast, cold coil steel is processed at room temperature, offering enhanced surface finish and tighter tolerances, making it suitable for precision applications like automotive and appliance manufacturing. Both types cater to different industrial needs and specifications.</p></p>
