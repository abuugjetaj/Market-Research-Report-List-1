<p><ul><li>HFFS Machines</li><li>VFFS Machines</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The packaging machines for pouches can be categorized into three main types: Horizontal Form Fill Seal (HFFS) machines, Vertical Form Fill Seal (VFFS) machines, and others. HFFS machines operate horizontally, creating pouches from flat films and filling them with products, suitable for larger items. VFFS machines work vertically, forming pouches from a film roll, ideal for granules and powders. Other types may include specialty machines for unique packaging needs or designs, catering to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
