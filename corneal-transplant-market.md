<p><p>The corneal transplant market encompasses various types of procedures aimed at restoring vision. Penetrating keratoplasty involves the complete replacement of the cornea with donor tissue. Endothelial keratoplasty focuses on replacing only the damaged inner layer of the cornea. Descemet Stripping Automated Endothelial Keratoplasty is a refined method of endothelial keratoplasty, using precise techniques for better outcomes. Corneal limbal stem cell transplant restores stem cells to repair damaged corneal epithelium. Other procedures include superficial keratectomy and lamellar keratoplasty, addressing specific corneal issues.</p></p>
