<p><p>The slit valves market is categorized into three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Pneumatic slit valves utilize compressed air for operation, offering rapid actuation and low maintenance, making them ideal for cleanroom environments. Hydraulic slit valves employ fluid pressure for powerful and precise control, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Electric slit valves are driven by electric actuators, providing accurate positioning and automation capabilities. Each type serves distinct industrial sectors based on operational needs, efficiency, and control requirements.</p></p>
