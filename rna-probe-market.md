<p><p>The RNA probe market includes several key types: cRNA probes are synthesized from complementary RNA and used for hybridization studies, allowing researchers to analyze gene expression. Single-stranded cDNA probes are derived from complementary DNA and are employed in applications such as gene cloning and expression monitoring. Oligonucleotide probes, composed of short DNA or RNA sequences, are utilized for specific target identification in various assays, providing sensitivity and specificity in detecting genetic material. Each type caters to distinct research needs.</p></p>
