<p><p>The Plane Blazed Holographic Gratings market is segmented into two main types: reflection gratings and transmission gratings. Reflection gratings are designed to reflect light, making them ideal for applications like laser beam splitting and optical filtering, providing high efficiency and specific wavelength targeting. In contrast, transmission gratings allow light to pass through, commonly used in spectrometers and imaging systems. Both types offer unique advantages in manipulating light, serving various industries such as telecommunications, spectroscopy, and optical sensing.</p></p>
