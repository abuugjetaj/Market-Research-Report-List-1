<p><strong>The Viral Vectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Retroviral Vectors</li><li>Lentiviral Vectors</li><li>Adenoviral Vectors</li><li>Herpes Simplex Virus Vectors</li><li>Chimeric Viral Vectors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The viral vectors market includes several types used for gene therapy and vaccine development. Retroviral vectors integrate into the host genome but are limited to dividing cells. Lentiviral vectors, a subtype of retroviruses, can target non-dividing cells, offering broader applications. Adenoviral vectors efficiently deliver genes but do not integrate into the genome, reducing long-term effects. Herpes simplex virus vectors are useful for neural applications. Chimeric viral vectors combine features from different viruses for enhanced delivery. Other types include modified viral systems for specialized applications.</p></p>
