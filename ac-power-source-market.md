<p><p>The AC power source market is primarily divided into two types: linear AC power sources and PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) AC power sources. Linear AC power sources offer stable and low-distortion output, making them ideal for sensitive applications like testing and research. In contrast, PWM AC power sources utilize rapid switching techniques to achieve desired output voltage and frequency levels more efficiently, catering to a wider range of applications, including industrial and commercial use, where compactness and energy efficiency are crucial.</p></p>
