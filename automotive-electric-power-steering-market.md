<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Column Assist EPS</li><li>Single-Pinion Assist EPS</li><li>Dual-Pinion Assist EPS</li><li>Rack Assist EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) market comprises several types, each with unique mechanisms. Column Assist EPS utilizes a motor mounted on the steering column to support steering effort. Single-Pinion Assist EPS features a compact design that combines assistance with a single pinion gear. Dual-Pinion Assist EPS offers enhanced steering feedback and precision through two pinion gears. Rack Assist EPS directly assists the rack-and-pinion mechanism, providing efficient steering response and improved driving experience. Each type caters to varying vehicle and driver needs.</p></p>
