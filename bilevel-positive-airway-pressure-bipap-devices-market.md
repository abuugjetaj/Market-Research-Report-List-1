<p><strong>The Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Pressure BiPAP Device</li><li>Auto Adjusting BiPAP Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bilevel positive airway pressure (BiPAP) device market is categorized into two main types: Fixed Pressure BiPAP Devices and Auto-Adjusting BiPAP Devices. Fixed Pressure BiPAP Devices deliver a constant pressure for inhalation and exhalation, making them suitable for patients with consistent airway resistance. In contrast, Auto Adjusting BiPAP Devices automatically modify airway pressures based on patient needs throughout the night, providing personalized therapy and enhanced comfort. Both types are essential for managing sleep apnea and other respiratory conditions effectively.</p></p>
