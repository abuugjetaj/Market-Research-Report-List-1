<p><p>The lift truck market comprises various classes of forklifts tailored for specific applications. Class 1 forklifts are electric motor riders designed for indoor use, providing zero emissions and high maneuverability. Class 2 trucks, or narrow-aisle trucks, facilitate tight space operations with specialized designs. Class 3 trucks are electric powered and ideal for indoor warehousing, offering maneuverability in limited spaces. Class 4 and 5 forklifts are internal combustion trucks suitable for outdoor and heavy-duty applications, balancing power and durability for various environments.</p></p>
