<p><p>Distributed fiber optic sensors for the power and utility market utilize optical fibers to monitor physical parameters over long distances. The two primary types are single-mode and multimode fibers. Single-mode fibers have a smaller core, allowing light to travel directly, which results in higher precision and longer range for sensing applications. In contrast, multimode fibers have a larger core that permits multiple light paths, making them suitable for shorter distances and less critical applications. Both types enhance infrastructure monitoring and maintenance.</p></p>
