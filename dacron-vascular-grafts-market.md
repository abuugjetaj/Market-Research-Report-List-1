<p><p>Dacron vascular grafts are synthetic materials used in vascular surgeries, distinguished by their porosity levels. Low porosity grafts have tightly woven fibers, providing reduced permeability and enhanced resistance to blood flow, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Conversely, high porosity grafts feature a more open structure, promoting tissue ingrowth and integration, suitable for applications where healing is critical. The market for these graft types caters to varying surgical needs, balancing durability and biological compatibility.</p></p>
