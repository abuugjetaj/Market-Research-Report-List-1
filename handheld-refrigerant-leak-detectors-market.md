<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld refrigerant leak detectors market can be categorized into two primary types: infrared and semiconductor detectors. Infrared detectors utilize advanced technology to identify refrigerant leaks based on changes in infrared light absorption, offering high sensitivity and selectivity for various gases. Semiconductor detectors rely on the electrical conductivity changes in semiconductor materials when exposed to refrigerants, providing a cost-effective solution for leak detection. Both types cater to diverse industrial and commercial applications, ensuring prompt maintenance and safety in HVAC systems.</p></p>
