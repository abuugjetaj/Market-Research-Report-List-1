<p><p>The drain snake market consists of two primary types: manual and electric drain snakes. Manual drain snakes are operated by hand, typically featuring a flexible metal cable that users insert to clear blockages mechanically. They are cost-effective and suitable for small clogs. In contrast, electric drain snakes are powered by a motor, allowing for greater power and efficiency in tackling stubborn blockages. They are ideal for larger plumbing tasks, used by professionals for quicker, more effective results.</p></p>
