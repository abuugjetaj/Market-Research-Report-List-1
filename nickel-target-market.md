<p><p>The nickel target market consists of two primary types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat surfaces used in sputtering processes for uniform deposition, ideal for applications requiring precise thin films. In contrast, rotating targets enhance material distribution during the sputtering process, resulting in improved efficiency and longer lifespan due to more even wear. Both types cater to industries such as electronics, optics, and photovoltaics, each serving distinct needs depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
