<p><p>The interposer market is categorized into three main types: 2D, 2.5D, and 3D interposers. </p><p>2D interposers offer a flat surface for integrating multiple chips on a single substrate, primarily used in simpler applications. </p><p>2.5D interposers incorporate vertical connections, allowing for higher bandwidth and faster communication between chips while enhancing performance. </p><p>3D interposers stack chips vertically, significantly saving space and improving efficiency through direct interconnection, catering to advanced applications in high-performance computing and telecommunications.</p></p>
