<p><p>The niobium metal market comprises several key types: ferroniobium, niobium oxide, and niobium metal itself. Ferroniobium, a niobium-iron alloy, is primarily used as an additive in steel production to enhance strength and corrosion resistance. Niobium oxide serves as a precursor for various niobium compounds and is utilized in electronics and ceramics. Pure niobium metal, known for its high melting point and superconducting properties, is essential in aerospace, medical applications, and advanced technologies, driving diverse industrial demands.</p></p>
