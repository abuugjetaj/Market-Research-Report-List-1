<p><p>The industry check valves market encompasses various types designed to control fluid flow in piping systems. Ball valves use a spherical disc to regulate flow, while butterfly valves utilize a rotating disk for efficient space-saving operation. Gate valves allow full opening or closing, ideal for on/off control. Globe valves manage flow rates through an internal baffle, whereas plug valves employ a tapered or cylindrical plug. Diaphragm valves use a flexible diaphragm for precise flow control, and other valves include specialized designs for unique applications.</p></p>
