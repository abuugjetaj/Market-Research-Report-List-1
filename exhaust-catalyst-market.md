<p><strong>The Exhaust Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tightly Coupled Catalyst</li><li>Bottom Tray Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The exhaust catalyst market consists of various types designed for managing emissions in vehicles. Tightly Coupled Catalysts are integrated closely with the engine, allowing for quicker light-off temperatures and improved conversion efficiencies of pollutants. In contrast, Bottom Tray Catalysts are mounted beneath the vehicle, optimizing space and reducing thermal losses while still effectively converting harmful emissions. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing environmental performance and meeting stringent regulatory standards in the automotive industry.</p></p>
