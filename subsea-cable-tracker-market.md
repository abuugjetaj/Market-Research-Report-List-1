<p><ul><li>Tone Detector Subsea Cable Tracker</li><li>Pulse Induction Subsea Cable Tracker</li><li>Mag-gradiometer Subsea Cable Tracker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea cable tracker market features three primary types: Tone Detector Subsea Cable Trackers, which use audio frequencies to locate cables; Pulse Induction Subsea Cable Trackers, which send short electromagnetic pulses to detect buried cables; and Mag-gradiometer Subsea Cable Trackers, which measure magnetic fields to identify cable positions. Each type offers unique detection methodologies suited for varying environmental conditions, depths, and cable materials, providing operators with effective solutions for cable management and maintenance in underwater environments.</p></p>
