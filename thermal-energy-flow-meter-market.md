<p><strong>The Thermal Energy Flow Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Insertion</li><li>Portable</li><li>Inline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal energy flow meter market comprises three primary types: insertion, portable, and inline flow meters. Insertion flow meters are designed for easy installation into existing pipelines, enabling effective monitoring without complete system shutdown. Portable flow meters offer flexibility, allowing for temporary measurements in various locations, making them ideal for maintenance and troubleshooting. Inline flow meters are permanently installed within the fluid system, providing continuous and precise monitoring of thermal energy in processes, ensuring operational efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
