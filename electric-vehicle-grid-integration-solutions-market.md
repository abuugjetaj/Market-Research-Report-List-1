<p><p>Electric Vehicle Grid Integration Solutions encompass vital market types like Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) and Virtual Power Plants (VPP). V2G allows electric vehicles to discharge stored energy back to the grid, enabling flexible energy management and supporting grid stability. Conversely, VPP aggregates distributed energy resources, including EVs, to optimize electricity generation and consumption. Both solutions enhance grid resilience, facilitate renewable energy integration, and provide economic benefits to vehicle owners and utilities by balancing supply and demand more effectively.</p></p>
