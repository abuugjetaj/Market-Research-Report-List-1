<p><p>The infant milk powder market comprises several types catering to diverse nutritional needs. Cow's milk-based formula, the most common, mimics breast milk and is suitable for healthy infants. Soy-based formula is ideal for those with lactose intolerance or milk allergies. Protein hydrolysate-based formula features extensively broken down proteins for infants with allergies or digestive issues. Specialty formulas address specific medical needs, such as metabolic disorders or premature birth, ensuring tailored nutrition for vulnerable populations.</p></p>
