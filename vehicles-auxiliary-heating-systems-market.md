<p><ul><li>Fuel Based Auxiliary Heaters</li><li>Electrical / PTC Auxiliary Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicles Auxiliary Heating Systems market encompasses two primary types: Fuel-Based Auxiliary Heaters and Electrical/ PTC Auxiliary Heaters. Fuel-Based Auxiliary Heaters operate using various fuels, providing efficient heating while leveraging the vehicle’s existing fuel supply. In contrast, Electrical or PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) Auxiliary Heaters rely on electrical resistance to generate heat, offering quick and efficient warming, particularly in hybrid and electric vehicles. Both systems enhance passenger comfort and improve engine performance in cold conditions.</p></p>
